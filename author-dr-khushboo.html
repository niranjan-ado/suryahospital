Dr. Khushboo Priya

Gynaecologist & IVF Specialist
MBBS (Hons.), MS (ObGyn)

Dr. Khushboo Priya is a Gold Medalist and a distinguished specialist in Obstetrics and Gynaecology. With a deep commitment to women's health, she has spearheaded the development of advanced fertility treatments in Sahibganj, bringing hope to countless families.

Her approach to medicine is rooted in empathy. She understands that issues regarding fertility and high-risk pregnancies are not just physical challenges, but emotional journeys. By combining evidence-based protocols with compassionate counselling, she ensures every patient feels heard and supported.

Expertise

Dr. Priya specializes in the management of High-Risk Pregnancies, ensuring safety for both mother and child during complex deliveries. She is also a pioneer in bringing modern Infertility Treatments (IVF & IUI) to the region, reducing the need for patients to travel to metropolitan cities for conception assistance.

Articles & Insights

Dr. Priya is an active advocate for health education. She regularly writes about debunking medical myths to empower women with the right knowledge.
