Niranjan Ramamurthy

Founder & Digital Strategist, Adostrophe

The technology used by global giants shouldn't be out of reach for local businesses. Whether it's a digital ecosystem for a rural hospital or high-precision documentation for an industrial plant, my goal is to make enterprise-grade tools accessible and actionable.

As the Director of Adostrophe, I focus on bridging the gap between physical infrastructure and digital presence. My work balances technical precision with visual storytelling, helping organizations transition from static operations to data-rich digital environments.

Building the Surya Ecosystem

When the leadership at Surya Hospital approached us, they didn't just need a website—they needed a way to connect a fragmented healthcare landscape. I took on the responsibility of architecting and building their entire digital footprint.

By developing the platforms for the Hospital, the Nursing College, and the Plaza Hotel using modern web standards, we created a lightweight, high-performance network. This ensures that critical healthcare information is instantly accessible to patients in Sahibganj, even on low-bandwidth connections.

Visualizing the Physical World

Beyond web architecture, a significant portion of my work involves creating Digital Twins. From industrial plants to hospitality suites, these models allow stakeholders to inspect and manage facilities remotely.

We have been pioneering the use of Matterport in India, helping clients move beyond static photography. This technology is not just about visuals; it is about transparency. For a project like Surya Hospital, accuracy isn't a luxury—it's a necessity for patients trusting us with their care.
