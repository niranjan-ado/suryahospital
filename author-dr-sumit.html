Dr. Sumit Kumar

Urologist
MS, FIAGES, MCh (Urology)

Dr. Sumit Kumar is a highly skilled Urologist known for his expertise in minimally invasive endourology. He combines technical precision with a patient-centered approach to treat complex urinary and kidney conditions.

His presence in Sahibganj ensures that patients suffering from acute kidney issues or prostate disorders receive gold-standard care without the burden of traveling to distant cities.

Clinical Focus

Dr. Kumar's expertise covers the diagnosis and treatment of:

  • Kidney Stones: Advanced laser and endoscopic removal techniques.
  • Prostate Health: Management of BPH and other prostate conditions.
  • Urinary Tract Infections (UTI): Comprehensive care for recurrent infections.

Urology Consultation

Expert care for kidney and bladder health is just a call away.
